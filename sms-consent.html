SMS Consent & Opt-In

How we collect consent and how you can manage your SMS preferences

How We Collect Consent

ANX Home Healthcare and ANX Hospice Care collects SMS consent during the patient intake and referral process. When a referral is created in our system, patients and family members provide their phone number and explicitly agree to receive text message notifications about their referral status.

Example: Referral Intake Form — SMS Consent Section

I agree to receive text message notifications from ANX Home Healthcare and ANX Hospice Care regarding referral status updates, appointment reminders, and care coordination. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out at any time. Reply HELP for help. See our Privacy Policy and Terms and Conditions.

Healthcare partners (hospitals, physician offices, skilled nursing facilities) consent to SMS notifications when they register as referral partners and provide contact phone numbers through our partner onboarding process.

What Messages You'll Receive

Our SMS notifications include:

  • Referral received confirmation
  • Insurance verification status updates
  • Clinical assessment scheduling and reminders
  • Admission and care plan notifications
  • Care coordination alerts for healthcare partners

Important Information

Message Frequency: Varies based on referral activity. Typically 2–10 messages per referral.

Message & Data Rates: Standard rates from your wireless carrier may apply.

No Purchase Required: Consent is not a condition of any purchase.

Your phone number will not be shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

How to Opt Out

You can stop receiving SMS messages at any time. Simply reply to any message with the keyword STOP. You will receive a one-time confirmation that you have been unsubscribed.

How to Get Help

Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or contact us directly:

Email: privacy@anxlife.com
Phone: (555) 010-0000

SMS Keywords

Keyword What Happens
STOP Unsubscribe from all SMS notifications. You'll receive a confirmation.
HELP Receive a help message with contact information and program details.
START Re-subscribe to SMS notifications after opting out.
YES Confirm opt-in to SMS notifications.

Re-Subscribing

If you previously opted out and wish to receive messages again, text START to our number. You can also contact your care coordinator to re-enable SMS notifications on your referral.

Compatible Carriers

Our SMS program is compatible with all major U.S. wireless carriers, including AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, Sprint, U.S. Cellular, and others. If your carrier is not supported, you may not receive messages.
